Mariam Khanfar, a PhD Candidate in the Griffith Lab, won a travel award to present a lightning talk and poster at the 2024 ITCR Annual Meeting in Indianapolis, Indiana. She presented her work titled, “Advancing Precision Oncology: Collaborative Efforts in the Curation and Classification of Somatic Cancer Variants by ClinGen Somatic and CIViC.”
